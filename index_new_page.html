About Us

Born in San Luis Obispo, used countrywide.

Backyard BIG finds its origins from a couple college kids in San Luis Obispo, CA. Our goal was to make a couple bucks with some extra house space and to have a good time while doing so.

Six months later, college students around the country are benefiting from our app, and in turn, more local bands are getting the exposure they deserve.

We are pumped with the positive outpour thus far, and the future Backyard BIG is bright!

Go BIG,

- The Backyard BIG Team
